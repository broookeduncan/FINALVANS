Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)


Do you ship internationally?


No, Vans only ships orders in the United States of America.


What is your return policy?

Customers have up to 90 days to return or exchange items.


How to I cancel an order made online?

Unfortunately, you may not cancel your order, however, you may return the item in-store.


What days do you offer delivery?

We offer delivery Monday through Friday. We do not deliver on holidays.
